Density

These maps display the housing unit density in zip codes of four urban areas. The four "urban areas," San Francisco - Oakland - Heyward (Bay Area), New York City, Chicago, and Los Angeles, are defined by the census. Housing unit data is from the 2017 5-year American Community Survey. The coloring is scaled according to the four urban areas in question.
